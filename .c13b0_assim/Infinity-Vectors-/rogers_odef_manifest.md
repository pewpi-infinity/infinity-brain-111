## 1. Introduction

The Omnidimensional Epistemological Framework aims to explore the multifaceted nature of knowledge. It helps to identify and connect different types of knowledge in a holistic manner.

## 2. Dimensions of Knowledge

### 2.1 Spatial Dimension
This dimension considers knowledge as it relates to physical space. It explores how spatial understanding influences knowledge acquisition and dissemination.

### 2.2 Temporal Dimension
Here, knowledge is viewed through the lens of time. This dimension examines how historical contexts affect the interpretation and value of knowledge.

### 2.3 Conceptual Dimension
This includes abstract concepts and their roles in knowledge systems. It focuses on the interaction between various ideas and their implications.
